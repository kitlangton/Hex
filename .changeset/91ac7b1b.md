HexCoach M2 UI: Settings → "Pronunciation Coach" section with provider picker, secure API-key entry (saved to Keychain, "Test key" runs a real round-trip), L1 / target-accent / goal pickers, threshold slider, "delete audio after analysis" toggle, and a privacy footer. Menu-bar gets a green dot when the coach is enabled and a "Pronunciation Coach" entry that opens a popover window showing the latest feedback (score, summary, top issues, wins) and recent sessions. macOS notifications fire on successful feedback ("Pronunciation tip") and once per session on the first error. Coach remains off by default.
