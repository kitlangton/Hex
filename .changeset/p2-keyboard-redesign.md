Redesign the iOS dictation keyboard into two letter-free, swipeable panels with a page-dot indicator (#P2-2, #P2-5). Panel 1 (Dictate) has the accent mic, an animated waveform while capturing, a tinted "mic hot · MM:SS left" session pill, and a globe/space/backspace/return row, rendering all six keyboard states in light and dark mode. Panel 2 (Controls) adds a caret trackpad, delete-word, undo/redo, and a punctuation cluster — no letters; the globe still hands off to the system keyboard.
