Coach now runs against `gemini-3.1-flash-lite` and analyzes audio only — Hex's local transcript is no longer sent to the LLM. The default prompt explicitly tells the model "you do not have a pre-supplied transcript — rely entirely on what you hear", and `{{TRANSCRIPT}}` is gone (no placeholders at all). The popover still shows Hex's local ASR as "You said" for context, but it's display-only.
