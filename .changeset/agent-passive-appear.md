The Agent Plugins voice window no longer steals keyboard focus when a hook makes it appear. A card that pops up while you're typing in your editor now appears passively — your keystrokes keep going to the editor. Press your agent-window hotkey (or tap the card) to "engage" it and focus the reply field; press the hotkey again to dismiss. Manually summoning the window still focuses it immediately.
