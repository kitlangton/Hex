Coach UI is now a real menu-bar popover. Dropped SwiftUI MenuBarExtra in favor of an NSStatusItem + NSPopover wired directly in HexAppDelegate. Left-click the status icon to toggle a vibrancy-backed popover anchored to the menu bar (no focus stolen from the active app); right-click for the menu (Check for Updates, Copy Last Transcript, Pronunciation Coach On/Off, Show Coach, Settings, Quit). When a recording finishes and the coach starts analyzing, the popover auto-presents anchored to the icon and you can keep typing in whatever app you were in.
